I'm a Senior Associate – Marketplace Operations at Global Bees, working on the operational backbone that keeps marketplace channels running at scale. Previously at CURRYiT, I managed end-to-end D2C and B2B fulfilment across platforms like Blinkit, Zepto, Swiggy Instamart, and Amazon — building dashboards and automations that cut manual work by hours daily.

I also build the tools that make that process lighter — Google Apps Script automations, tracking sheets, and small dashboards — whenever a manual process becomes repetitive enough to fix properly.

Outside of ops, I'm curious about technology that solves real business problems. I've shipped full-stack apps (React + Firebase + Supabase) and believe the best operations person is one who can also write the automation that makes ops faster.
