What I actually do

I'm an Assistant Manager – Business Operations at CURRYiT, where I manage end-to-end D2C order fulfilment and B2B logistics across platforms like Blinkit, Zepto, Swiggy Instamart, BigBasket, Amazon, and FirstClub. Day to day, that means dispatch planning, vendor and warehouse coordination, and tracking KPIs like OFD delays, RTO reasons, and missing GRNs.

I also build the tools that make that process lighter — Google Apps Script automations, tracking sheets, and small dashboards — whenever a manual process becomes repetitive enough to fix properly.
